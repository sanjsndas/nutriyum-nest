NutriyumNest Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2024

At NutriyumNest, we are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how it is used, the circumstances under which it may be shared, and the choices you have regarding your data. We operate NutriyumNest with a philosophy of transparency and respect for your personal space, aiming to provide a safe and enriching online environment where wellness meets every bite. By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services to you. This includes:

a. Personal Data

Personal Data refers to information that can be used to identify you individually. This may include, but is not limited to:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, postal address, phone number. This is typically collected when you register for an account, subscribe to our newsletter, participate in our community forums, or contact us directly.
  • Account Information: Username, password (encrypted), and other registration details.
  • Demographic Information: Age, gender, dietary preferences (e.g., vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free), and cooking experience levels, which you may voluntarily provide to help us tailor content.
  • Communications Data: Records of your correspondence with us, including emails, chat messages, and feedback.
  • User-Generated Content: Recipes you share, comments you post in our community, success stories, and any other content you voluntarily submit to the site.

b. Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when you access or use NutriyumNest. This data may include:

  • IP Address: Your Internet Protocol address, which can be used to infer your general location.
  • Browser Type and Version: Information about the web browser you are using.
  • Pages Visited: The specific pages you visit on NutriyumNest, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages.
  • Referring/Exit Pages: The website you came from and the website you go to after leaving NutriyumNest.
  • Device Information: Type of device (e.g., desktop, mobile, tablet), operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Clickstream Data: Information about your navigation path through our website, including interactions with features and content.

c. Tracking & Cookies Data

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our Service and hold certain information. Cookies are small data files placed on your device. Tracking technologies used are also beacons, tags, and scripts to collect and track information and to improve and analyze our Service.

  • Session Cookies: Used to operate our Service.
  • Preference Cookies: Used to remember your preferences and various settings.
  • Security Cookies: Used for security purposes.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to serve you with advertisements that may be relevant to you and your interests.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages are visited most often, and if they get error messages from web pages. These cookies don't collect information that identifies a visitor. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and therefore anonymous. It is only used to improve how our website works.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service. For more detailed information on cookies, please refer to our dedicated Cookies Policy page.

2. How We Use Your Information

NutriyumNest uses the collected data for various purposes, primarily to enhance your experience and provide relevant services:

  • To Provide and Maintain Our Service: This includes operating the website, managing your account, and delivering the services you request, such as recipe access and community features.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: We use your preferences and usage data to tailor content, recommend recipes, and provide nutrition tips that are most relevant to your interests and dietary needs.
  • To Improve Our Service: We analyze usage patterns to understand how our website is used, identify areas for improvement, and develop new features and content. This helps us make NutriyumNest more intuitive and valuable.
  • To Communicate with You: We may use your contact information to send you newsletters, updates, promotional offers, and respond to your inquiries and feedback. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • To Monitor Usage: We track user activity to detect and prevent technical issues, fraud, and abuse, ensuring the security and integrity of our platform.
  • For Research and Analytics: We may use anonymized or aggregated data for internal research, statistical analysis, and trend identification to better understand our audience and market.
  • To Enforce Our Terms and Conditions: To protect our rights, property, or safety, and that of our users or the public, as required or permitted by law.
  • For Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests from public authorities, and resolve disputes.

3. Sharing Your Information

NutriyumNest respects your privacy and will only share your information under specific circumstances:

a. With Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our Service ("Service Providers"), to provide the Service on our behalf, to perform Service-related services, or to assist us in analyzing how our Service is used. These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose. Examples include hosting providers, analytics services, email marketing platforms, and customer support tools. We ensure that all service providers adhere to strict data protection standards.

b. For Business Transfers

If NutriyumNest is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your Personal Data may be transferred. We will provide notice before your Personal Data is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

c. For Legal Requirements

NutriyumNest may disclose your Personal Data in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation (e.g., court order, subpoena).
  • Protect and defend the rights or property of NutriyumNest.
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with the Service.
  • Protect the personal safety of users of the Service or the public.
  • Protect against legal liability.

d. With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when we have your explicit consent to do so. For example, if you choose to participate in a sponsored promotion or survey, we may share your information with the sponsoring partner.

e. Aggregated or Anonymized Data

We may share aggregated or anonymized information that does not directly identify you with third parties for various purposes, including research, analytics, marketing, and to improve our services. For example, we might share statistics on popular recipes or user demographics.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is paramount to us. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your Personal Data when you enter, submit, or access your personal information. These measures include:

  • Encryption: Using SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission to protect information during transit.
  • Access Controls: Restricting access to your personal data to authorized personnel only, who are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Data Minimization: Collecting only the data that is necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy.
  • Regular Security Audits: Periodically reviewing our security practices and systems to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.
  • Secure Storage: Storing data on secure servers with robust physical and electronic safeguards.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. We encourage you to also take steps to protect your personal information, such as using strong, unique passwords and being cautious about sharing personal details online.

5. Data Retention

NutriyumNest will retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your Personal Data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations (for example, if we are required to retain your data to comply with applicable laws), resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements and policies.

We will also retain Usage Data for internal analysis purposes. Usage Data is generally retained for a shorter period, except when this data is used to strengthen the security or to improve the functionality of our Service, or we are legally obligated to retain this data for longer time periods.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location and applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR for European users or CCPA for California residents), you may have certain rights regarding your Personal Data:

  • The Right to Access: You have the right to request copies of your Personal Data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The Right to Rectification: You have the right to request that NutriyumNest correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request that we complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • The Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You have the right to request that NutriyumNest erase your Personal Data, under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request that NutriyumNest restrict the processing of your Personal Data, under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Object to Processing: You have the right to object to NutriyumNest’s processing of your Personal Data, under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Data Portability: You have the right to request that NutriyumNest transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
  • The Right to Withdraw Consent: If we are relying on your consent to process your Personal Data, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of any processing carried out before you withdraw your consent.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@nutriyumnest.com. We may require you to verify your identity before processing your request.

Our Service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 ("Children").

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your Children have provided us with Personal Data, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services. This includes, but is not limited to, external websites linked within recipes, advertisements, or community discussions. Your interactions with these third-party sites are governed by their respective privacy policies.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

We will let you know via email and/or a prominent notice on our Service, prior to the change becoming effective and update the "last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

NutriyumNest will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy and no transfer of your Personal Data will take occur to an organization or a country unless there are adequate controls in place including the security of your data and other personal information.
